More On FoodFacts

Currently, many individuals are unaware of the food sources they put into their body. While nutrition facts are placed on many food items, the information provided is very limited, and in turn is not completely reliable. We want to transmit as much information as we can about food items and food sources to people, so they are able to not only see nutritional information and ingredients but also allergens, ingredient origins, any specific labels important for consumers, and even the environmental impact for the large group of people who are really interested in the impact of a product, including such details like the carbon footprint.
